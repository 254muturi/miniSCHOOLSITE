About Our School

**Our School's Journey** Established in 1900 by Mr. Ogolla, Fadhili Group of Schools has been a beacon of education in our community. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a thriving institution, nurturing young minds to reach their full potential. With a focus on academic excellence and character development, our school continues to inspire generations of learners. Join us on this exciting journey of learning and discovery!

Our School Vision

To be a premier institution of academic excellence.

Our Mission

At Fadhili Group of Schools, our mission is to provide a nurturing and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning. We are committed to empowering our students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to become responsible global citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Core Values

Our core values at Fadhili Foundation encompass fostering a culture of life-long learning, upholding integrity and accountability in the pursuit of excellence, embracing innovativeness to ensure quality and productivity, demonstrating social responsibility through collaborative efforts to address global concerns, and promoting academic freedom by encouraging open exchange of ideas and knowledge.
