WELCOME TO FADHILI FOUNDATION GROUP OF SCHOOLS

FADHILI FOUNDATION school is a co-educational school in Nairobi, Kenya. We teach children from age 3 to 14, following the National Curriculum of Kenya. We are a member of the FADHILI FOUNDATION GROUP OF SCHOOLS .The FADHILI FOUNDATION has succeeded in building a name as a reputable school offering quality and holistic education, meeting the needs and requirements of its stakeholders, including students, parents, and the government. Driven by our motto 'striving for excellence,' FADHILI FOUNDATION aims to pursue excellence in the provision of quality Kindergarten, Primary, and Junior High School education.
